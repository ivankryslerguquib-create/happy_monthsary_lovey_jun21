A Letter for You

Written on a night when I couldn't stop thinking about you

My Dearest Love,

There are moments in life when words feel too small to hold what the heart wants to say. Yet here I am, trying anyway — because some things deserve to be written down, even imperfectly, even if they pale beside the real thing.

You came into my life like the first breath of spring after a long winter. Before you, I didn't know what I was waiting for. Now, I cannot imagine a single tomorrow without you in it. You are my sunrise and my starlight, my calm and my adventure.

I love the way you laugh when something catches you off guard. I love how your hand feels in mine, like it was always meant to be there. I love that you see me — truly see me — and still choose to stay.

This website, this small gesture, is my way of saying: you are cherished. You are celebrated. You are loved more deeply than any words could ever capture.

Forever & Always, Yours
